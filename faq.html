How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our homepage. Fill in your details, including your email address and a secure password. Once you've completed the form, click "Create Account" and you're all set!

How often should I work out?

The frequency of your workouts depends on your fitness goals and current fitness level. Generally, we recommend 3-5 workouts per week for most people. However, it's essential to listen to your body and allow for proper rest and recovery between sessions.

How can I track my calorie intake?

FitConnect offers a built-in calorie tracking feature. Simply log your meals and snacks in the app, and it will automatically calculate your calorie intake. You can also sync FitConnect with popular nutrition apps for seamless tracking across platforms.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. You can manage your payment options in your account settings.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. Follow the link in the email to create a new, secure password for your account.
